About Smart Irrigation Advisor

A sustainable agriculture solution developed for TSA Software Development competition 2025.

Our Mission

Agriculture accounts for approximately 70% of global freshwater withdrawals. With increasing water scarcity due to climate change and population growth, optimizing irrigation practices is crucial for sustainable agriculture.

Smart Irrigation Advisor helps farmers and gardeners make data-driven irrigation decisions that conserve water while maintaining or improving crop yields.

Environmental Impact

If just 10% of farms adopted precision irrigation like our system recommends:

  • Could save ~3.5 trillion liters of water annually
  • Reduce energy use for water pumping
  • Decrease fertilizer runoff
  • Maintain or improve crop yields
